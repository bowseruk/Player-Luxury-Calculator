Luxury Player Tax Calculator

Explanation

The luxury player tax is charged on any spending on players over the tax floor. All spending above the tax floor is taxed at the tax rate. The quantity of the spending below the floor is not taxed.

Using This Page

Add or remove the player count to the number required. The minimum number of players is 1.

When the data is input, the total cost of players will automatically calculate. The required tax will also be calculated. The tax floor and tax rate can be adjusted if required.
